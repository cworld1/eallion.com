1、复制 PC 端优惠券链接：[http://taoquan.taobao.com/coupon/unify_apply.htm?sellerId=xxxxxxxxxx&activityId=xxxxxxxxx](http://taoquan.taobao.com/coupon/unify_apply.htm?sellerId=xxxxxxxxxx&activityId=xxxxxxxxx)
